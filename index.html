About Me

A Communicator at Heart

I am Ankita Dubey, a media strategist and narrative consultant with a strong foundation in journalism, public relations, and digital storytelling. My journey began with an M.A. in Mass Communication from Banaras Hindu University (BHU), 2010–2012 — one of India's most prestigious institutions for communication studies.

My career spans some of India's most respected media and digital organisations, including Zee Media — where I honed skills in broadcast journalism, editorial planning, and audience engagement — and Digicliff Solutions, where I operated at the intersection of digital content, brand communication, and campaign strategy.

Today, through Pressoria, I channel that experience into a boutique consultancy that serves founders, startups, and enterprises who need clear, powerful, and authentic communication. I believe every brand has a story worth telling — my job is to make the world listen.
